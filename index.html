Our Mission

Our mission is to preach the Word of God and bring people into membership of God's family; to teach the Word of God to enhance freedom; promote Christian maturity and bind the people to God for service, and to live the Word of God to demonstrate the new life in Christ to the World, and ensure the security of the believer.
